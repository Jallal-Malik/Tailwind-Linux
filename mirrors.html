How to mirror Linux Mint

The following archives can be mirrored. The table below indicates the rsync address for each archive as well as the frequency and an estimation of the disk space they require.

ARCHIVE RSYNC ADDRESS REQUIRED FREQUENCY SIZE
ISO images pub.linuxmint.com::pub Once a day 200GB
Linux Mint repositories rsync-packages.linuxmint.com::packages Twice a day 25GB

If you are mirroring some of these archives at the required frequency, please contact us by email at root@linuxmint.com to tell us about your mirror.
